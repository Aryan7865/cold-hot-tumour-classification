## 1. Executive summary

We analyse **bulk RNA-seq** from **The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA)** for **colorectal cancer**: **TCGA-COAD** (colon adenocarcinoma) as the **discovery** cohort and **TCGA-READ** (rectal adenocarcinoma) as a **fully independent external validation** cohort. We assign each tumour an **immune phenotype** — **Hot**, **Cold**, or **Intermediate** — using **ssGSEA** and **ESTIMATE** scores and **within-cohort tertiles** (not pathology slides). We then apply the full BT3041 toolkit: **distribution checks**, **PCA / ICA / MDS / t-SNE / UMAP**, **k-means, hierarchical, and DBSCAN clustering**, a **battery of per-gene hypothesis tests** with **Benjamini–Hochberg and Bonferroni** correction, **six supervised classifiers** with **stratified 5-fold cross-validation**, **Kaplan–Meier, log-rank, Cox, and PLS** survival analyses, and **external validation** by training on COAD and testing on READ. **Key headline:** strong discovery performance (~0.79 balanced accuracy in CV) and **meaningful generalisation** on READ (~0.74 balanced accuracy, n=109), with **thousands of statistically robust DE genes** enriched for **MHC class II and cytotoxic immune biology**.

---

## 2. Biological background and clinical motivation

**Hot tumours** show dense immune infiltration (e.g. CD8+ T cells, NK cells) and interferon-gamma signalling; many respond to **immune checkpoint blockade**. **Cold tumours** are immune-excluded or deserted and often fail checkpoint therapy. **Intermediate** captures tumours between these extremes.

**Why RNA-seq?** Bulk tumour RNA-seq measures **thousands of transcripts** per sample. It is increasingly available in translational research. If a **computational signature** recovers hot vs cold structure, it could in principle support stratification **without** full multiplex IHC on every block — subject to validation limitations we state explicitly.

## 3. Course alignment (BT3041 rubric)

The instructor workflow asks for: **dataset identification**, **exploratory analysis** (PCA/UMAP, plots, clustering), **hypothesis-driven DE** with **FDR**, **classification/regression**, and **validation/interpretation**. Our project maps as follows:

- **Exploratory:** PCA, ICA, MDS, t-SNE, UMAP; immune-score histograms; clustering with silhouette, dendrogram, DBSCAN.
- **Hypothesis-driven:** Welch t, ANOVA, Kruskal–Wallis, Mann–Whitney U, F-test of variances; **BH and Bonferroni** on each p-value family; volcano and heatmaps; chi-squared on clinical tables.
- **Classification/regression:** Six classifiers + CV; logistic/SVM as linear boundaries; tree ensembles; XGBoost; PLS vs survival time; Cox regression.
- **Validation:** **Held-out cohort** (READ) never used in training; metrics vs READ ssGSEA labels.
- **Interpretation:** Top DE genes overlap **published immune programmes** (antigen presentation, cytotoxicity).

## 5. Data acquisition and raw matrices

**Script:** `01_data_acquisition.py`

- Uses the **NCI GDC REST API** to list and download **STAR-counts** gene expression files and **clinical** supplements per project.
- **Engineering note:** single-shot bulk `tar.gz` downloads often **truncated** on long links. We implemented **batched downloads** (e.g. 50 file IDs per request) with **retries and exponential backoff**, then merge parsed tables.
- **Parser:** builds `data/raw/<cohort>/expression_counts.tsv` with **HGNC gene symbols** as rows, samples as columns; handles duplicate `gene_name` rows (e.g. PAR regions) and **replicate aliquots** with disambiguated column names.

## 7. Immune scoring and Hot / Cold / Intermediate labels

**Script:** `03_immune_scoring.py`

1. Run **ssGSEA** (`gseapy`) on immune-related gene sets plus **ESTIMATE** immune/stromal signatures.
2. Rank samples by **ESTIMATE immune score** (primary ranking variable in our pipeline narrative).
3. **Tertile split within each cohort:** top third **Hot**, bottom third **Cold**, middle **Intermediate** (`HOT_QUANTILE` 0.66, `COLD_QUANTILE` 0.33 in `config.py`).

**Label counts** (`outputs/tables/final_summary.csv`):

| Cohort | n | Hot | Cold | Intermediate |
|--------|---|-----|------|----------------|
| TCGA-COAD | 294 | 100 | 97 | 97 |
| TCGA-READ | 109 | 37 | 36 | 36 |

**Critical honesty for viva:** labels are **computational**, not pathologist “ground truth.” All supervised metrics measure agreement with **ssGSEA+ESTIMATE tertiles**, not IHC.

## 8. Train vs validation — memorise this table

| Role | Cohort | What happens |
|------|--------|----------------|
| **Discovery + model selection** | **TCGA-COAD** (294) | All DE, DR, clustering, survival on COAD; **5-fold stratified CV** picks best classifier by **mean balanced accuracy** |
| **External validation** | **TCGA-READ** (109) | **Never used in training or CV.** Gene list aligned to training; missing genes **zero-filled**. Frozen best pipeline evaluated vs READ’s own ssGSEA labels |

**External metrics** (`external_validation_summary_TCGA-READ.csv`): **RandomForest** — accuracy **~0.743**, balanced accuracy **~0.744**, macro-F1 **~0.742**. Random 3-class guessing ≈ **33%** accuracy → **~74% is a large margin**.

## 9. Dimensionality reduction

**Script:** `04_dimensionality_reduction.py`

- **PCA**, **FastICA**, **MDS** (metric and non-metric), **t-SNE**, **UMAP**.
- Non-linear methods often initialised from **PCA** coordinates for stability.
- **PCA variance (COAD):** PC1 ≈ **32.5%**, PC2 ≈ **9.4%**, PC1+PC2 ≈ **42%** of variance (`pca_variance_TCGA-COAD.csv`; up to 50 PCs stored).

**Interpretation:** Hot and Cold often separate along leading components; Intermediate occupies the **bridge** between extremes — consistent with a **continuous immune axis** discretised into tertiles.

## 10. Clustering and agreement with labels

**Script:** `05_clustering.py`

- **k-means** (k sweep with **silhouette**), **Ward hierarchical** (dendrogram), **DBSCAN** on PCA space.
- **Benchmark:** Adjusted Rand Index (**ARI**) and Adjusted Mutual Information (**AMI**) vs ssGSEA labels.

**Example (COAD, `clustering_metrics_TCGA-COAD.csv`):** **k-means k=3** → ARI **~0.114**, AMI **~0.119**, silhouette **~0.137**. **Moderate** agreement is expected: tertiles impose crisp boundaries on a **continuous** biology.

## 11. Hypothesis testing and multiple testing

**Script:** `06_hypothesis_testing.py`

**Per gene (COAD):**

- Welch **t-test** (Hot vs Cold)
- **One-way ANOVA** (three groups)
- **Kruskal–Wallis** (non-parametric omnibus)
- **Mann–Whitney U** (Hot vs Cold)
- **F-test** of variance (Hot vs Cold)

**Multiple testing:** **Benjamini–Hochberg** (`*_bh`) and **Bonferroni** (`*_bonf`) applied **per test family** in `dge_TCGA-COAD.csv`.

**High-confidence DE (`dge_sig_TCGA-COAD.csv`):** BH q<0.05 on Hot–Cold t-test **and** **|log₂FC| > 1** — **~2,269 genes** (line count minus header; regenerate PDF to refresh from disk).

**Clinical association:** `chi2_clinical_TCGA-COAD.csv` — e.g. vital status vs label: raw p **~0.026**, BH-adjusted **~0.077** → report as **suggestive trend**, not definitive after FDR.

**Figures:** volcano, top-40 heatmap in `outputs/figures/hypothesis/`.

## 12. Supervised machine learning

**Script:** `07_classification.py`

**Six models** (each a **Pipeline** with **SelectKBest(ANOVA, k=500)** + classifier unless noted):

1. **kNN-5** (with `StandardScaler`)
2. **SVM-linear** (probability=True for ROC)
3. **SVM-RBF**
4. **Logistic regression**
5. **RandomForest** (500 trees; `StandardScaler(with_mean=False)`)
6. **XGBoost** (500 trees; **label-encoder wrapper** for string class names)

**Evaluation:** stratified **5-fold CV** on COAD; **accuracy**, **balanced accuracy**, **macro-F1**. **Best by balanced accuracy:** **RandomForest** and **linear SVM** tie at **~0.785** mean balanced accuracy; RandomForest carried forward to **09_validation_external.py**.

**Why XGBoost is mid-pack:** aggressive univariate pre-screening + mostly linear separable structure favours **linear margins** and **shallow ensembles** over deep boosting in this setting.

## 13. Survival analysis

**Script:** `08_survival_analysis.py`

- **Time:** `days_to_death` where available else `days_to_last_follow_up`; **event** from `vital_status` (robust column matching for GDC’s nested field names).
- **Kaplan–Meier** by immune label; **multivariate log-rank** across three groups.
- **Cox:** penalised regression on selected ssGSEA cell-type scores (`cox_TCGA-COAD.csv`).
- **PLS:** expression → survival time (`pls_TCGA-COAD.csv`, PLS survival figure).

**Log-rank (COAD):** p **~0.081** — **not significant at α=0.05**; describe as **directional** (Cold trend worse) with **limited events/censoring**.

## 17. Limitations (say these without being asked)

- **Labels:** ssGSEA + ESTIMATE **proxy**, not IHC gold standard.
- **Validation:** READ is **same assay family** as COAD — not cross-technology (microarray) validation.
- **Survival:** log-rank **not significant** at 0.05 — do not oversell.
- **Causality:** expression associations are **not** therapeutic causal claims.
